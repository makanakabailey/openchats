## Key Components

- **Search Bar**: Find episodes by title, topic, or guest
- **Filter Sidebar**: Season, topic, year
- **Episode List**: Chronological with key info
- **Episode Card**: Artwork, title, date, duration, description
- **Action Buttons**: Play, discuss, show notes
- **Sort Options**: Newest, oldest, most popular

## Interactions

- Click play to open embedded player
- Filter updates list without reload
- Search with autocomplete suggestions
- Mobile: Filters in collapsible drawer
