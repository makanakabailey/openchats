## Key Components

- **Episode Header**: Title, artwork, metadata
- **Audio Player**: Full-featured embedded player with controls
- **Action Buttons**: Discuss, share, favorite
- **Tabs**: Show notes, transcript, resources, related
- **Timestamps**: Clickable chapter markers
- **Links Section**: Resources and sponsors mentioned
- **Related Discussions**: Direct links to community threads
- **More Episodes**: Carousel of related/next episodes

## Interactions

- Click timestamp to jump to that point
- Player persists when scrolling (sticky)
- Download episode for offline listening
- Share with timestamp link
- Transcript syncs with audio playback
