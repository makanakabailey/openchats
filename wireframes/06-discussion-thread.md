## Key Components

- **Thread Header**: Title, author, timestamp, action buttons
- **Original Post**: Full content with author profile and badges
- **Nested Replies**: Indented to show conversation hierarchy
- **Reaction Buttons**: Like, love, laugh, etc.
- **Host Badge**: Special indicator for official host replies
- **Reply Editor**: Rich text with media attachment options
- **Load More**: Pagination for long threads

## Interactions

- Click reply to open inline editor
- Reactions update in real-time
- Nested replies collapse/expand
- Sort by: Newest, Oldest, Most Liked
- Follow thread for notifications
